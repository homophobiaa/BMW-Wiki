The History of BMW

Explore BMW's journey from a small airplane engine manufacturer to a global leader in luxury vehicles and high-performance cars.

1916

Foundation of BMW

BMW was established as Bayerische Flugzeug-Werke (BFW), initially focused on producing airplane engines during World War I.

1923

First BMW Motorcycle

The R32, BMW’s first motorcycle, was launched, marking BMW’s entrance into the world of two-wheeled vehicles.

1933

First BMW Automobile

BMW produced its first car, the BMW 303, which introduced the signature kidney grille design.

1962

New Class of Sedans

The BMW New Class series was introduced, revitalizing BMW’s reputation with sportier, performance-focused sedans.

1972

BMW Motorsport GmbH

BMW’s performance division, now known as BMW M, was founded, bringing high-performance vehicles like the BMW M1 to the market.

1999

Introduction of the X Series

BMW entered the SUV market with the BMW X5, setting the stage for the popular X Series lineup of luxury SUVs.

2013

BMW i Series

The launch of the all-electric BMW i3 and hybrid BMW i8 marked BMW’s commitment to sustainable and eco-friendly vehicles.

2020s

Electric Future

BMW continues to expand its electric lineup with models like the iX and i4, aiming for a sustainable future with innovative electric vehicles.
