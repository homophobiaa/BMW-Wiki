BMW G42

The second-generation 2 Series Coupe (2021 - Present)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: 2.0L Inline-4, 3.0L Inline-6
  • Power: 184 - 374 hp
  • Torque: 300 - 500 Nm
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph)
  • 0-60 mph: 4.1 seconds (M240i xDrive)
  • Fuel Economy: 5.9 - 8.5 L/100 km (48 - 28 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2021 - Present
  • Body Style: 2-door Coupe
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive (xDrive on select models)
  • Weight: 1,560 - 1,800 kg

About the BMW G42

The BMW G42 2 Series Coupe represents the pinnacle of compact performance coupes. Combining stunning design, cutting-edge technology, and BMW's iconic driving dynamics, the G42 is built for enthusiasts who value style and performance.

The G42 is powered by a choice of efficient and powerful engines, with the M240i xDrive setting benchmarks in its class. Its sporty proportions and advanced features make it a standout in the premium compact segment.

Key Features

  • Performance: Enhanced aerodynamics, turbocharged engines, and precision handling.
  • Technology: BMW iDrive 7, digital cockpit, and advanced driver assistance systems.
  • Design: Aggressive styling with a low stance and wide track.
  • Safety: Active safety systems, including collision warning, adaptive cruise control, and lane keeping assist.

Conclusion
