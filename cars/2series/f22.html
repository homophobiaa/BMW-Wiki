BMW F2X

The second-generation 1 Series and 2 Series (2011 - 2019)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: 1.5L Inline-3, 2.0L Inline-4, 3.0L Inline-6
  • Power: 109 - 340 hp
  • Torque: 180 - 500 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual, 8-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph)
  • 0-60 mph: 4.2 seconds (M2 Competition)
  • Fuel Economy: 4.0 - 8.5 L/100 km (57 - 28 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2011 - 2019
  • Body Style: 5-door hatchback (F20), 3-door hatchback (F21), Coupe (F22), Convertible (F23)
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive (xDrive on select models)
  • Weight: 1,300 - 1,600 kg

About the BMW F2X

The BMW F2X series, encompassing the F20 and F21 1 Series along with the F22, F23 2 Series, represents a celebrated era of compact rear-wheel-drive vehicles. Known for their exceptional driving dynamics, these models hold a special place among enthusiasts.

Featuring turbocharged engines, refined interiors, and a variety of body styles, the F2X lineup provides options for both practicality and performance, making it a versatile choice for a wide audience.

Key Features

  • Performance: Turbocharged engines and precise handling, optimized for driving enthusiasts.
  • Versatility: A variety of body styles catering to different preferences, from hatchbacks to coupes.
  • Technology: Advanced infotainment systems, driver assistance features, and premium audio options.
  • Safety: Equipped with modern safety systems like stability control, adaptive cruise control, and lane departure warnings.
  • Design: Distinctive BMW styling with aggressive lines and signature kidney grille.

Technical Information

The BMW F2X series boasts a range of engines and configurations. Below are key details for notable variants:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • B38: 1.5L Inline-3, 109 hp, used in 116i
  • B48: 2.0L Inline-4, up to 224 hp, used in 125i
  • B58: 3.0L Inline-6, 340 hp, used in M140i
  • N55: 3.0L Inline-6, 370 hp, used in M2
  • B47: 2.0L Diesel Inline-4, 190 hp, used in 120d

Conclusion

The BMW F2X series remains a favorite among those seeking a compact car with exceptional driving dynamics, premium features, and BMW's signature performance. Whether as a daily driver or a weekend toy, the F2X delivers on all fronts.
