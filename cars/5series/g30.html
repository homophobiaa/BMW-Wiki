BMW G30

The seventh-generation 5 Series (2017 - Present)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-4, Inline-6, V8, Plug-in Hybrid
  • Power: 184 - 617 hp (M5)
  • Torque: 290 - 750 Nm (M5)
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic, 7-speed dual-clutch (M5)
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph) for the M5
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.4 seconds (M5)
  • Fuel Economy: 5.0 - 12.5 L/100 km (47 - 19 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2017 - Present
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive, All-Wheel Drive (xDrive models)
  • Weight: 1,600 - 2,000 kg

About the BMW G30

The BMW G30 represents a new era for the 5 Series, bringing a more dynamic design, cutting-edge technology, and exceptional performance. It blends luxury and sportiness in a way that attracts both driving enthusiasts and those who value comfort.

The G30 M5 is a true performance machine with a 4.4L V8 twin-turbo engine, while the plug-in hybrid variants focus on efficiency without compromising on power.

Image Gallery

Key Features

  • Performance: M5 model powered by a 4.4L V8 twin-turbo delivering 617 hp.
  • Design: Sleek and modern design with sharper lines and improved aerodynamics.
  • Technology: Advanced iDrive system, active safety features, gesture control, and more.

Technical Information

The BMW G30 offers a variety of engine configurations for different needs and preferences:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • B46: Inline-4, used in 520i, 530i.
  • B58: Inline-6, used in 540i, 530d.
  • S63: 4.4L V8 twin-turbo, used in M5, producing 617 hp.
  • PHEV: Plug-in Hybrid, used in 530e, offering great efficiency and performance.

Conclusion

The BMW G30 sets a new benchmark in the 5 Series lineup, offering a combination of modern luxury, advanced technology, and thrilling performance, especially in the M5 variant. Its sleek design and powerful engine choices ensure its place as one of the best in its class.
