BMW E31

The first-generation BMW 8 Series (1989 - 1999)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, V12
  • Power: 286 - 380 hp
  • Torque: 400 - 550 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual, 4-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph)
  • 0-100 km/h: 6.3 seconds (840Ci)
  • Fuel Economy: 12.0 - 16.0 L/100 km (20 - 14 mpg)
  • Production Years: 1989 - 1999
  • Body Style: Coupe
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive
  • Weight: 1,700 - 1,800 kg

About the BMW E31

Technical Information

The BMW E31 was offered with several different engines during its production run:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • M30: Inline-6, used in 840i.
  • M60: V8, used in 850i.
  • M70: V12, used in 850Ci, 850CSi.

Conclusion

The BMW E31 remains an iconic and highly sought-after luxury sports coupe. With its mix of advanced features, exceptional performance, and timeless design, it stands as one of BMW's most impressive creations from the 1990s.
