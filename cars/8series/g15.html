BMW G15

The BMW 8 Series Convertible (2018 - Present)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, V12
  • Power: 335 - 617 hp
  • Torque: 500 - 850 Nm
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph)
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.9 seconds (M850i)
  • Fuel Economy: 10.5 - 14.0 L/100 km (22 - 16 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2018 - Present
  • Body Style: Convertible
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive, All-Wheel Drive (xDrive models)
  • Weight: 1,900 - 2,000 kg

About the BMW G15

The BMW G15 is the latest iteration of the BMW 8 Series, a luxurious convertible sports car that combines high-performance with open-air driving pleasure. First introduced in 2018, the G15 features a modern, aggressive design and cutting-edge technology that showcases BMW's commitment to excellence.

The G15 is available with a range of powerful engines, including inline-6, V8, and V12 configurations. The M850i variant stands out with its 617 hp, offering thrilling performance and an exceptional driving experience, all while maintaining the elegance and comfort expected from BMW's flagship coupe.

Conclusion
