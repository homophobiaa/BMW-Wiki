BMW X6

The Luxury Sports Activity Coupe (2008 - Present)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, Diesel, Hybrid
  • Power: 335 - 617 hp (X6 M)
  • Torque: 450 - 750 Nm
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic, 6-speed manual
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph) for X6 M
  • 0-100 km/h: 3.9 seconds (X6 M)
  • Fuel Economy: 7.5 - 14.0 L/100 km (31 - 16 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2008 - Present
  • Body Style: SUV Coupe, 5-door
  • Drive Type: All-Wheel Drive (xDrive)
  • Weight: 2,200 - 2,500 kg

About the BMW X6

The BMW X6, introduced in 2008, redefined the luxury SUV market with its bold coupe-like design combined with the practicality of an SUV. Known as the “Sports Activity Coupe,” the X6 blends sporty performance with high-end luxury, catering to drivers who want the best of both worlds.

It pioneered a new segment in the automotive world, offering a distinctive silhouette and dynamic driving experience while maintaining versatility and premium features.

Key Features

  • Performance: X6 M model offers a powerful 617 hp V8 engine, achieving thrilling speeds.
  • Design: Bold and aggressive coupe-like design with a spacious interior.
  • Technology: BMW’s iDrive system, driver assistance features, and a premium audio system.

Technical Information

The BMW X6 offers a variety of powerful engines and advanced technologies:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • B58: 3.0L Inline-6 Turbo, used in X6 xDrive40i and xDrive50i.
  • F96: 4.4L V8 Twin-Turbo, used in X6 M and X6 M50i.
  • F15: Diesel engine options available for various markets (X6 xDrive30d, xDrive40d).
  • F16: Hybrid powertrain available in select models.

Conclusion

The BMW X6 has become a standout vehicle in the luxury SUV category, offering sporty driving dynamics, aggressive styling, and a premium interior. Whether you’re looking for performance, luxury, or a unique design, the X6 provides it all, making it a perfect choice for enthusiasts who want something more than a traditional SUV.
