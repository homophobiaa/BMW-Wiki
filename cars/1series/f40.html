BMW F40

The third-generation 1 Series hatchback (2019 - Present)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: 1.5L Inline-3, 2.0L Inline-4, 2.0L Diesel
  • Power: 109 - 306 hp
  • Torque: 190 - 450 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual, 7-speed DCT, 8-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph)
  • 0-60 mph: 4.7 seconds (M135i xDrive)
  • Fuel Economy: 4.0 - 7.5 L/100 km (57 - 31 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2019 - Present
  • Body Style: 5-door hatchback
  • Drive Type: Front-Wheel Drive (AWD on select models)
  • Weight: 1,350 - 1,500 kg

About the BMW F40

The BMW F40 is the third generation of the 1 Series, marking a significant shift for the model lineup with its transition to a front-wheel-drive platform. Despite the change, the F40 maintains BMW's hallmark of dynamic driving performance and luxurious comfort.

Equipped with cutting-edge technology, a bold design, and a versatile range of engines, the F40 caters to a wide audience. The M135i xDrive, with its all-wheel-drive system and high-output engine, stands out as a favorite among enthusiasts.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Performance: Agile handling and a range of efficient yet powerful engines.
  • Innovative Technology: Includes BMW's latest iDrive system, digital cockpit, and advanced driver assistance features.
  • Practical Design: Spacious interior with a modern, premium feel, and a focus on passenger comfort.
  • Safety: Equipped with advanced safety features such as lane-keeping assist, adaptive cruise control, and collision warning systems.
  • Striking Design: Sleek, aerodynamic styling with the iconic kidney grille and LED lighting.

Technical Information

Conclusion

The BMW F40 represents a new era for the 1 Series, combining practicality, modern technology, and spirited driving dynamics. It appeals to those seeking a premium hatchback that delivers both style and performance for everyday use.
