About the BMW M4 G82 CSL

The BMW M4 G82 CSL is the pinnacle of lightweight engineering and dynamic performance. With its aerodynamic enhancements, precision-tuned suspension, and a powerful 3.0L inline-6 twin-turbo engine, the G82 CSL delivers an unparalleled driving experience. Combining track-focused performance with everyday usability, it redefines what a high-performance coupe can achieve.
