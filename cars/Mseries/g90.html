About the BMW M5 G90

The 2025 BMW M5 G90 redefines high-performance sedans with a plug-in hybrid system combining a 4.4L twin-turbo V8 and electric motor, delivering 535 kW and 1,000 Nm of torque. It accelerates from 0–100 km/h in 3.5 seconds and reaches a top speed of 305 km/h. Despite its increased weight (2,435 kg), it offers impressive agility. Key features include multiple drive modes (Hybrid, Electric, eControl), an electric-only range of 60 km, illuminated kidney grilles, and an updated interior with a Curved Display, Bowers & Wilkins audio, and four-zone climate control. Starting at $119,500, it launches globally in November 2024.
