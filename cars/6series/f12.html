BMW F12

The sixth-generation 6 Series Coupe (2011 - 2018)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, V12 (M6)
  • Power: 320 - 600 hp
  • Torque: 450 - 700 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual, 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph) for the M6
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.1 seconds (M6)
  • Fuel Economy: 9.0 - 14.0 L/100 km (26 - 16 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2011 - 2018
  • Body Style: Coupe, Convertible
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive, All-Wheel Drive (xDrive models)
  • Weight: 1,800 - 1,900 kg

About the BMW F12

The BMW F12 is the sixth-generation 6 Series Coupe, introduced in 2011. It represents the modern iteration of BMW's luxurious grand tourer, offering a sophisticated design, high performance, and cutting-edge technology. The F12 features a more refined and muscular look compared to its predecessor, with dynamic performance in the M6 variant.

The M6 model, powered by a 4.4L twin-turbocharged V8 engine, is a true powerhouse, delivering exhilarating speed and agility on the road.

Key Features

  • Performance: M6 model powered by a 4.4L twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 600 hp.
  • Design: A sleek and athletic design with luxury and elegance in mind.
  • Technology: iDrive system, adaptive suspension, and advanced driver assistance systems.

Technical Information

The BMW F12 offers a range of advanced engine options, including the powerful M6 with its high-performance twin-turbo V8:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • N55: 3.0L Inline-6, used in 640i, 640i xDrive.
  • N63: 4.4L V8, used in 650i, 650i xDrive.
  • S63: 4.4L twin-turbo V8, used in the M6, producing 600 hp.

Conclusion

The BMW F12 is a perfect balance of elegance and performance. With its bold design, luxurious interior, and the high-performance M6 variant, the F12 solidified its place as a top-tier grand tourer. Its combination of power and refinement continues to appeal to driving enthusiasts and those seeking comfort on long journeys.
