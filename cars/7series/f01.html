BMW F01

The seventh-generation 7 Series (2008 - 2015)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, V12, Hybrid
  • Power: 235 - 560 hp
  • Torque: 330 - 750 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed automatic, 8-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph) for the 760i
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.6 seconds (760i)
  • Fuel Economy: 9.0 - 12.0 L/100 km (26 - 19 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2008 - 2015
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive, All-Wheel Drive (xDrive)
  • Weight: 1,800 - 2,100 kg

About the BMW F01

The BMW F01 is the seventh generation of the BMW 7 Series, produced from 2008 to 2015. It represented a major step forward in both technology and luxury, combining cutting-edge features with exceptional performance.

Notable for its introduction of the 8-speed automatic transmission and the available hybrid variant, the F01 also featured innovations in comfort and safety, including night vision, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control.

Image Gallery

Key Features

  • Performance: The 760i is powered by a V12 engine producing 560 hp.
  • Technology: Advanced safety and convenience features, including night vision, adaptive cruise control, and lane departure warning.
  • Comfort: Luxurious interior with advanced seat adjustment, high-end audio, and a premium feel.

Technical Information

The BMW F01 offers a variety of powerful engine configurations:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • N53: Inline-6, used in 730i, 740i.
  • N63: V8, used in 750i, 740i xDrive.
  • M73: V12, used in 760i, producing 560 hp.
  • PHEV: Hybrid variant in 740e, with electric motor assistance.

Conclusion

The BMW F01 brings together luxury, technology, and performance in a way that few cars can. With advanced features like adaptive cruise control, night vision, and a range of powerful engines, the F01 continues to be a benchmark for high-end sedans. Its blend of comfort, innovation, and performance makes it a true modern classic.
