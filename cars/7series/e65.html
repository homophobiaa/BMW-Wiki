BMW E65

The sixth-generation 7 Series (2001 - 2008)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-6, V8, V12
  • Power: 231 - 500 hp
  • Torque: 330 - 700 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed automatic, 6-speed manual
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph) for the 760i
  • 0-100 km/h: 5.4 seconds (760i)
  • Fuel Economy: 11.5 - 15.0 L/100 km (20 - 15 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2001 - 2008
  • Body Style: Sedan
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive, All-Wheel Drive (xDrive)
  • Weight: 1,800 - 2,000 kg

About the BMW E65

The BMW E65 is the sixth generation of the BMW 7 Series, produced between 2001 and 2008. It introduced a bold and modern design with a focus on luxury, technology, and performance. The E65 was known for its futuristic iDrive system, which revolutionized car technology at the time.

Equipped with powerful engines, including the V12 in the 760i model, the E65 combined high-end luxury with impressive performance. The E65 also featured advanced safety features, such as active cruise control and a reinforced body structure.

Key Features

  • Performance: 760i model powered by a V12 engine producing 500 hp.
  • Technology: Introduced the iDrive system, advanced cruise control, and active safety features.
  • Design: Bold and modern styling with a focus on comfort and luxury.

Technical Information

The BMW E65 offered a range of powerful engines and configurations:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • M54: Inline-6, used in 730i, 735i.
  • M62: V8, used in 745i, 750i.
  • M73: V12, used in 760i, producing 500 hp.
  • N62: V8, used in 745i, 750i.

Conclusion

The BMW E65 remains a defining model for the BMW 7 Series, thanks to its groundbreaking technology and bold design. The 760i, with its powerful V12 engine, is still remembered as one of the most luxurious and high-performing sedans of its time. The E65's combination of comfort, performance, and innovation continues to make it a favorite among enthusiasts.
