About the BMW G11

The BMW G11 is the sixth generation of the BMW 7 Series, produced from 2015 to 2022. It features a luxury sedan with advanced technologies, including the BMW iDrive 7.0 system, gesture controls, and optional V12 and hybrid engines.

Notable for its lightweight construction using carbon fiber reinforced plastic (CFRP) and aluminum, the G11 offers improved performance and fuel efficiency. The high-performance M760Li with a V12 engine and BMWâ€™s xDrive all-wheel-drive system further elevate its capabilities.

Technical Information

The BMW G11 features a range of powerful engines, ensuring both performance and efficiency:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • B48: Inline-4, used in 730i, 740i.
  • B57: Inline-6, used in 740d, 740i xDrive.
  • N63: V8, used in 750i, 750Li xDrive.
  • M74: V12, used in M760Li, producing 601 hp.
  • PHEV: Plug-in hybrid variant in 740e, combining electric and petrol engines.

Conclusion

The BMW G11 redefines luxury and performance in the flagship 7 Series sedan. It offers cutting-edge technology, impressive powertrain options, and an incredibly smooth ride. The M760Li variant stands as the performance pinnacle with its V12 engine and exquisite luxury features, making it a standout in the high-performance luxury sedan market.
