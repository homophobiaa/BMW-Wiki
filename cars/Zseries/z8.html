About the BMW Z8

The BMW Z8 is a timeless roadster, combining retro-inspired design with modern performance. It was produced as a limited edition from 2000 to 2003, drawing design cues from the legendary 507 roadster of the 1950s. The Z8 featured a powerful 4.9L V8 engine, capable of delivering 400 hp and propelling the car from 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.7 seconds.

Designed by Henrik Fisker, the Z8’s aesthetics were enhanced by its long bonnet, wide stance, and curvaceous body. The car's driving experience was further enhanced with its precision handling and a near-perfect 50/50 weight distribution, making it one of BMW’s finest roadsters.
