BMW Z4

The Ultimate Roadster (2002 - Present)

Car Specifications

  • Engine Options: Inline-4, Inline-6, Hybrid (Z4 M40i, Z4 30i)
  • Power: 184 - 382 hp (Z4 M40i)
  • Torque: 320 - 500 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual, 8-speed automatic
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (155 mph)
  • 0-100 km/h: 4.4 seconds (Z4 M40i)
  • Fuel Economy: 7.5 - 10.0 L/100 km (32 - 24 mpg)
  • Production Years: 2002 - Present
  • Body Style: Roadster, Coupe
  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive, xDrive (AWD)
  • Weight: 1,350 - 1,650 kg

About the BMW Z4

The BMW Z4 is a stylish and high-performance roadster that blends luxury with agility. First introduced in 2002, it quickly became popular among driving enthusiasts. The Z4 offers exhilarating driving dynamics with its sharp handling, powerful engine options, and sleek design. Whether in convertible or coupe form, it offers a thrilling driving experience that has evolved through multiple generations.

Over the years, the Z4 has seen a variety of performance upgrades, including the introduction of the M40i trim, which pushes the limits of performance with a turbocharged 3.0L inline-six engine, reaching 0-100 km/h in just 4.4 seconds.

Key Features

  • Performance: M40i model with 382 hp and a 0-100 km/h time of 4.4 seconds.
  • Design: Modern roadster design with a driver-focused cockpit and sleek body lines.
  • Technology: iDrive system, available Apple CarPlay, and advanced safety features.

Technical Information

The Z4 has offered a variety of engines and configurations over the years:

Engine Codes and Iterations

  • BMW B46: 2.0L Inline-4, used in Z4 20i, 30i.
  • BMW B58: 3.0L Inline-6, used in Z4 M40i, producing 382 hp.
  • BMW PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid): A future hybrid variant expected in upcoming models.

Conclusion

The BMW Z4 is a standout in the world of luxury roadsters. Its dynamic handling, powerful engine options, and eye-catching design make it a car that offers both thrills and style. The Z4 continues to evolve and remain relevant, combining performance with cutting-edge technology and luxurious details.
